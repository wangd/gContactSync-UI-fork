Class Detail
com.gContactSync.ContactConverter()
Converts contacts between Thunderbird's format (a 'card') and the Atom/XML representation of a contact. Must be initialized before the first use by calling the init() function. NOTE: The first 6 screennames of a contact from Google are stored as: _AimScreenName, TalkScreenName, ICQScreenName, YahooScreenName, MSNScreenName and JabberScreenName for compatibility with gContactSync 0.1b1 and the default type for those textboxes.
