com.gContactSync.Overlay()
Checks if the authentication token is present and valid. If so, it starts everything up and synchronizes the contacts. Otherwise it shows the login window.

Updates the Card View pane boxes and headers for whether or not they should be visible based on additional attributes added by gContactSync.
