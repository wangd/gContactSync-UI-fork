Welcome to the new mozdev.org project site for gContactSync!

gContactSync is an extension that synchronizes contacts from a Google (Gmail) account with an Address Book in Thunderbird 2 or 3.
I originally wrote this for my Google Summer of Code project in 2008 and now work on it as a hobby and learning exercise.
Version 0.2 is out, and please report any errors you find, or suggest enhancements.

Downloads

I post stable versions of gContactSync on addons.mozilla.org.
You may also download the newest stable or testing version on the Downloads page.
